Segmented Video Player

For playing vertical or ultra-wide (horizontal) video on a standard 16:9 monitor

This player splits vertical video or ultra-wide video into 'segments' and allows you to quickly switch between them on the fly, while the video is playing.

Controls

Press Up/Down to switch between segments. Press Left/Right to scrub video +/- 1 second Press SPACE to play/pause video. Press M to mute/unmute video. Press C to toggle control-bar on/off
